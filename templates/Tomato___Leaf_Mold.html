Tomato - Leaf Mold

About the Disease

Leaf mold is a fungal disease caused by Passalora fulva, primarily affecting tomato plants under humid conditions.

Symptoms

Symptoms include yellowing of the upper leaves and olive-green to brown spots on the undersides, which can lead to leaf drop.

Solutions

To manage Leaf Mold:

  • Improve air circulation around plants.
  • Apply fungicides specifically for leaf mold.
  • Remove and destroy infected leaves.
