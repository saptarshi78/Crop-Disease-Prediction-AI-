Corn (Maize) - Healthy

About the Condition

A healthy corn plant displays vibrant green leaves and strong stalks, yielding high-quality ears of corn.

Characteristics

Healthy plants have a robust root system and good resistance to pests and diseases.

Maintenance

To maintain plant health:

  • Provide adequate nutrients and water.
  • Monitor for pests and diseases regularly.
  • Practice crop rotation to maintain soil health.
