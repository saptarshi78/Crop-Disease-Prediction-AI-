Pepper - Bacterial Spot

About the Disease

Bacterial spot of pepper is caused by Xanthomonas campestris. The bacteria cause dark, water-soaked lesions on leaves, stems, and fruits.

Symptoms

Symptoms appear as small, dark spots on leaves and can cause defoliation, impacting yield.

Solutions

To control bacterial spot:

  • Use copper-based fungicides for prevention.
  • Implement crop rotation and avoid overhead irrigation.
  • Remove and destroy infected plant debris.
