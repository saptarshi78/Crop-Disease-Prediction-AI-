Cherry - Healthy

About the Condition

A healthy cherry tree is free from disease and produces high-quality cherries.

Characteristics

Healthy trees have lush green leaves and abundant fruit set.

Maintenance

To maintain tree health:

  • Provide sufficient water and nutrients.
  • Prune to ensure good air circulation.
  • Regularly monitor for pests and diseases.
