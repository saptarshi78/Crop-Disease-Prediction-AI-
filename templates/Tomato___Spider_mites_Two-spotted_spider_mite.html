Tomato - Spider Mites (Two-spotted Spider Mite)

About the Pest

Two-spotted spider mites are tiny pests that feed on tomato plants, causing yellow stippling and reduced vigor.

Symptoms

Symptoms include yellowing leaves, webbing on the underside of leaves, and defoliation.

Solutions

To manage spider mites:

  • Introduce predatory mites to control the population.
  • Use miticides if infestations are severe.
  • Maintain good air circulation to prevent mite buildup.
