Tomato - Early Blight

About the Disease

Early blight is caused by the fungus Alternaria solani, characterized by dark, concentric spots on leaves.

Symptoms

Symptoms include dark spots on older leaves and premature defoliation, reducing yield and quality.

Solutions

To manage early blight:

  • Apply fungicides as soon as symptoms appear.
  • Practice crop rotation and remove infected plant debris.
  • Provide adequate spacing between plants for air circulation.
