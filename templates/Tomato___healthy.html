Tomato - Healthy

About the Condition

A healthy tomato plant has lush green foliage and produces high-quality fruit with no signs of disease.

Characteristics

Healthy plants exhibit robust growth and good fruit set, with vibrant green leaves.

Maintenance

To maintain plant health:

  • Provide adequate water and nutrients.
  • Monitor regularly for pests and diseases.
  • Practice crop rotation and proper sanitation.
