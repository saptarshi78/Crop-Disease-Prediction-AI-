Corn (Maize) - Common Rust

About the Disease

Common rust is caused by the fungus Puccinia sorghi, resulting in reddish-brown pustules on leaves.

Symptoms

Symptoms include small, raised pustules that burst and release spores, leading to reduced photosynthesis and yield.

Solutions

To manage common rust:

  • Plant resistant corn varieties.
  • Apply fungicides when necessary.
  • Practice crop rotation to disrupt the disease cycle.
