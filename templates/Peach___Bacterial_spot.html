Peach - Bacterial Spot

About the Disease

Bacterial spot on peach is caused by Xanthomonas arboricola and leads to lesions on leaves and fruits.

Symptoms

Symptoms include dark, water-soaked spots on leaves, which can lead to defoliation and fruit drop.

Solutions

To control bacterial spot:

  • Use copper-based bactericides for prevention.
  • Avoid overhead irrigation to reduce leaf wetness.
  • Prune trees to improve air circulation.
