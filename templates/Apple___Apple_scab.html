Apple - Apple Scab

About the Disease

Apple scab is caused by the fungus Venturia inaequalis, leading to dark, olive-green lesions on leaves and fruit.

Symptoms

Symptoms include curled leaves and dark spots, which can lead to defoliation and reduce fruit quality.

Solutions

To control apple scab:

  • Apply fungicides early in the season.
  • Promote good air circulation around trees.
  • Remove fallen leaves to reduce overwintering spores.
