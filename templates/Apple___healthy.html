Apple - Healthy

About the Condition

A healthy apple tree shows no signs of disease and produces high-quality fruit.

Characteristics

Healthy apple trees have vibrant green leaves, robust growth, and good fruit set.

Maintenance

To maintain tree health:

  • Provide adequate water and nutrients.
  • Prune regularly to promote air circulation.
  • Monitor for pests and diseases frequently.
