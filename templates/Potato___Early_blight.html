Potato - Early Blight

About the Disease

Early blight is caused by the fungus Alternaria solani, which affects potato plants, leading to significant yield loss.

Symptoms

Symptoms include dark, concentric lesions on leaves, which can cause defoliation and reduce tuber quality.

Solutions

To control Early Blight:

  • Apply fungicides at the first sign of disease.
  • Practice crop rotation to prevent recurrence.
  • Remove and destroy infected plant debris.
