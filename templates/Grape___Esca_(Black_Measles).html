Grape - Esca (Black Measles)

About the Disease

Esca is a complex disease caused by multiple fungi affecting the vascular system of grapevines.

Symptoms

Symptoms include leaf discoloration, premature leaf drop, and black streaks in the wood.

Solutions

To control Esca:

  • Prune and destroy infected vines.
  • Practice good vineyard management to promote vine health.
  • Ensure proper drainage to prevent excess moisture.
