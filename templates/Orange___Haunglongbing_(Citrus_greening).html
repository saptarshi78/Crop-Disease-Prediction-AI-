Orange - Huanglongbing (Citrus Greening)

About the Disease

Huanglongbing is caused by bacteria transmitted by the Asian citrus psyllid and affects the entire citrus tree.

Symptoms

Symptoms include yellowing leaves, small and bitter fruit, and dieback of branches.

Solutions

To manage Huanglongbing:

  • Remove and destroy infected trees to prevent spread.
  • Control the psyllid population with insecticides.
  • Maintain tree health through proper nutrition and care.
