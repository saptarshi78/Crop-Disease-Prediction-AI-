Potato - Late Blight

About the Disease

Late blight is caused by the oomycete Phytophthora infestans, a serious disease affecting potatoes and tomatoes.

Symptoms

Symptoms include dark lesions on leaves and stems, which can quickly lead to plant death and total crop loss.

Solutions

To manage Late Blight:

  • Use resistant potato varieties.
  • Apply fungicides regularly during wet weather.
  • Practice good sanitation by removing infected plants.
