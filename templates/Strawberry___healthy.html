Strawberry - Healthy

About Healthy Strawberries

Healthy strawberry plants have vibrant green leaves and produce fruit without any disease symptoms.

Symptoms of Health

Healthy strawberries will show no wilting, yellowing, or lesions on leaves.

Solutions

To maintain healthy strawberries:

  • Ensure adequate watering and drainage.
  • Mulch to retain moisture and suppress weeds.
  • Regularly inspect for pests and diseases.
