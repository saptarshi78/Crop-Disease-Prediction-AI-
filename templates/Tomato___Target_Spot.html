Tomato - Target Spot

About the Disease

Target spot is a fungal disease caused by Corynespora cassiicola, leading to brown lesions on leaves and fruit.

Symptoms

Symptoms include brown spots on leaves with concentric rings, defoliation, and lesions on fruit.

Solutions

To manage Target Spot:

  • Use resistant tomato varieties.
  • Apply fungicides as a preventative measure.
  • Remove and destroy infected plant material.
