Cherry - Powdery Mildew

About the Disease

Powdery mildew is caused by the fungus Sphaerotheca pannosa, characterized by a white powdery growth on leaves and fruit.

Symptoms

Symptoms include white fungal growth on leaves and stems, leading to stunted growth and reduced fruit quality.

Solutions

To manage powdery mildew:

  • Apply fungicides during early stages of infection.
  • Ensure adequate air circulation around trees.
  • Remove infected plant parts promptly.
