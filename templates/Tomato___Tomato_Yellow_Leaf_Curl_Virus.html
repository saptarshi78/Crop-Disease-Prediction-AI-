Tomato - Tomato Yellow Leaf Curl Virus

About the Disease

Tomato Yellow Leaf Curl Virus (TYLCV) is a viral disease transmitted by whiteflies, causing stunted growth and yellowing of leaves.

Symptoms

Symptoms include upward curling of leaves, yellowing between veins, and reduced fruit production.

Solutions

To manage TYLCV:

  • Use resistant tomato varieties.
  • Control whitefly populations with insecticides or biological agents.
  • Remove infected plants to prevent spread.
