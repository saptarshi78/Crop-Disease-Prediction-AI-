Apple - Black Rot

About the Disease

Black rot is caused by the fungus Guignardia bidwellii, resulting in black lesions on leaves and fruit.

Symptoms

Symptoms include black lesions on the fruit and leaf, leading to premature leaf drop and poor fruit quality.

Solutions

To manage black rot:

  • Use resistant apple varieties.
  • Apply fungicides during bloom and fruit development.
  • Practice proper sanitation and remove infected fruit.
