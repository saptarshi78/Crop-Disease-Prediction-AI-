Potato - Healthy

About Healthy Potatoes

Healthy potatoes exhibit robust growth, green foliage, and good tuber formation.

Symptoms of Health

Healthy potatoes will not show any disease symptoms such as wilting, yellowing, or lesions.

Solutions

To maintain healthy potatoes:

  • Ensure proper irrigation and nutrient supply.
  • Implement crop rotation to prevent disease buildup.
  • Regularly monitor for pests and diseases.
