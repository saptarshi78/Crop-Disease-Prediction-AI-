Strawberry - Leaf Scorch

About the Disease

Leaf scorch in strawberries is primarily caused by environmental stress and can be exacerbated by diseases such as bacterial wilt.

Symptoms

Symptoms include yellowing of leaf margins, wilting, and browning at the tips of leaves.

Solutions

To manage leaf scorch:

  • Ensure consistent watering to avoid drought stress.
  • Mulch to conserve soil moisture and regulate temperature.
  • Fertilize appropriately to prevent nutrient deficiencies.
