Tomato - Septoria Leaf Spot

About the Disease

Septoria leaf spot is caused by the fungus Septoria lycopersici, leading to small, dark spots on leaves.

Symptoms

Symptoms include yellowing leaves, leaf drop, and reduced yield.

Solutions

To manage septoria leaf spot:

  • Use resistant tomato varieties.
  • Apply fungicides as needed.
  • Practice crop rotation and proper sanitation.
