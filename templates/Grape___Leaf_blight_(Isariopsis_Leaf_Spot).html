Grape - Leaf Blight (Isariopsis Leaf Spot)

About the Disease

Leaf blight is caused by the fungus Isariopsis clavispora, which results in leaf spots and premature leaf drop.

Symptoms

Symptoms include small, dark, circular spots on leaves that can merge, leading to large areas of dead tissue.

Solutions

To control leaf blight:

  • Apply fungicides during the growing season.
  • Remove and destroy infected leaves to reduce spore spread.
  • Ensure good air circulation around the vines.
