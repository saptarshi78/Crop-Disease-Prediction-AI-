Tomato - Bacterial Spot

About the Disease

Bacterial spot is caused by Xanthomonas campestris, leading to dark, water-soaked spots on leaves and fruit.

Symptoms

Symptoms include dark, greasy-looking spots on leaves, causing defoliation and reduced yield.

Solutions

To manage bacterial spot:

  • Practice crop rotation and remove infected debris.
  • Apply copper-based bactericides.
  • Avoid overhead watering to minimize leaf wetness.
