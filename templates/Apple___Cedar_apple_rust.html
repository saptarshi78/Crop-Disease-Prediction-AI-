Apple - Cedar Apple Rust

About the Disease

Cedar apple rust is caused by the fungus Gymnosporangium juniperi-virginianae, affecting both apple and cedar trees.

Symptoms

Symptoms include bright yellow-orange spots on leaves, leading to significant leaf drop.

Solutions

To control cedar apple rust:

  • Apply fungicides during the growing season.
  • Remove cedar trees near apple orchards.
  • Plant resistant apple varieties.
