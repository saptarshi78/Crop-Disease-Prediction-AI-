Squash - Powdery Mildew

About the Disease

Powdery mildew is caused by fungal pathogens that thrive in warm, dry conditions, affecting squash plants.

Symptoms

Symptoms include white, powdery spots on leaves, which can lead to leaf yellowing and wilting.

Solutions

To control Powdery Mildew:

  • Apply sulfur or other fungicides.
  • Ensure good air circulation around plants.
  • Remove infected plant debris after harvest.
