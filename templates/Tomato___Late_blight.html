Tomato - Late Blight

About the Disease

Late blight is caused by the fungus Phytophthora infestans, resulting in dark spots on leaves and fruit.

Symptoms

Symptoms include water-soaked lesions on leaves and stems, leading to rapid plant decline.

Solutions

To manage late blight:

  • Apply fungicides preventively and at first sign of symptoms.
  • Ensure good air circulation and avoid overhead watering.
  • Remove and destroy infected plants immediately.
