Tomato - Tomato Mosaic Virus

About the Disease

Tomato mosaic virus is a highly infectious disease that causes mottling and mosaic-like patterns on leaves and fruits.

Symptoms

Symptoms include mottling, stunted growth, and yellowing of leaves. Fruits may also develop brown streaks.

Solutions

To manage Tomato Mosaic Virus:

  • Use virus-resistant tomato varieties.
  • Sanitize tools and equipment to prevent spread.
  • Remove and destroy infected plants.
